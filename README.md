## 🦷 Dental Clinic Management System – PHP & MySQL

## 📌 Project Overview
The **Dental Clinic Management System** is a full-stack web application built as a PGD final project, designed to digitize and streamline the day-to-day operations of a dental clinic.
The system integrates **clinical, laboratory, billing, and administrative workflows** into a single platform, improving efficiency, accuracy, and patient care.

---

## 🛠️ Features

### 👤 Patient Management
- Manage patient records and medical history
- Track complete patient history including visits, tests, and payments
- Search and filter patient records

### 📅 Appointment Scheduling
- Schedule and manage appointments
- Dentist allocation and availability tracking

### 🧪 Laboratory Management
- Handle lab test requests and results
- Upload and manage lab-related files

### 💰 Billing & Payments
- Generate lab bills and clinic bills
- Apply discounts and tax calculations
- Track payments and outstanding balances

### 📦 Inventory Management
- Track inventory items and stock levels
- Monitor usage and availability

### 🔐 Access Control
- Role-based access for admins, dentists, lab staff, and receptionists
- Secure authentication system

### 📊 Reporting
- Reporting dashboards for clinic operations
- Visual summaries of billing and patient data

---

## 🚀 Technologies Used
- **PHP** – Backend logic and server-side scripting
- **MySQL** – Database management
- **HTML, CSS, Bootstrap** – Frontend design and responsiveness
- **JavaScript** – Interactive UI components

---

## ⚙️ Core Functionalities
- Authentication & Role-Based Access Control
- CRUD Operations across all modules
- File Uploads for lab results and documents
- Automated Calculations for billing (tax, discounts, totals)
- Search & Filters across patient and record data
- Reporting Dashboards for operational insights

---

## 🎓 Project Context
Developed as a final project for the Postgraduate Diploma (PGD) program, focused on applying full-stack development skills to solve a real-world clinical management problem.
